Power

Probability of correctly rejecting the null hypothesis that p0 = p1, or R = 1, or ψ = 1 given n experimental patients studied, m controls per experimental subject, Type I error probability α, and a true alternative hypothesis expressed in terms of p0 and p1, R, or ψ, respectively.

p0 For retrospective studies, p0 is the probability of exposure in controls. In prospective studies, p0 is the event rate among controls.

p1 For retrospective studies, p1 is the probability of exposure in cases. In prospective studies, p1 is the event rate among cases.
